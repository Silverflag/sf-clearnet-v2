What's the goal?

We work to find ways to get information to people that is able to fit their needs while being able to respect their privacy. We want to tailor content to them without collecting any information on any remote server. We plan to collect information from the RSS feeds of various news and technology sites, and connect them together with our content. We plan to run mirrors of services that offer collections of valuable information so that people can access information, for free, with perfect privacy.

Privacy-First Systems

We are working to build a site were people are able to access information without using any of their information. We plan to be as transparent as possible with the data practices and are contructing ways of serving personalized content without us being able to access the users data.

Tech Insights

We plan to be a place were people are able to come to inform themselves about new techonlogy. There will be information about new programs that come out for privacy, changes made to old programs, and anything else that is helpful to the target demographic.

Open Knowledge

We plan to make a large amount of free and open knowledge without any restriction for people to access it. We will have mirrors of services that do this well already, and create our own services that we will attempt to more directly achieve the goals that we have in mind.
